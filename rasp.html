Error Reporting

If the shader isn't working, please make sure that your client has the latest Optifine, but is otherwise unmodded. Tons of mods mess with shaders' ability to do things and can cause massive issues.

If you meet the requirements, are unmodded, and issues still persist, send me a message! I'm usually around on Discord~
